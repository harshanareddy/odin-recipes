Rava Laddu

Description

This is a popular Indian dessert recipe.It is made of semolina, sugar, milk, coconut(optional).

Ingredients

  • Semolina
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Ghee
  • Cashew nuts
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Heat a large dry skillet over medium to medium-high heat. Add the semolina, and toast, stirring constantly. Stir in the sugar and cardamom while toasting until light brown and fragrant, about 10 minutes.
  2. Heat a small amount of the ghee in a separate skillet over medium heat. Add the cashews, and cook until lightly toasted, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the cashews to the semolina along with the remaining ghee. Gradually stir hot milk into the semolina until it is thick enough to pack into a ball.
  4. Remove from heat, and form palm-sized balls. Let cool and serve.
